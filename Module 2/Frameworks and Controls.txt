Difference between Framework and Control

Framework: Guideline to help building plans to mitigate risk and threats, they help the org
follow the laws and regulations compliance.

Control: Safeguards to deal with specific security risks, they are the measures used to lower
risk and threats to data and privacy.



Cyber Threat Framework (CTF)
 
 Provides a common language for communicating information about cyber threat activity. This
helps professionals to communicate more efficiently with each other and keep track of the constantly
evolving area that is cybersecurity.
