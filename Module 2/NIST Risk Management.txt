NIST (National Institute of Standards and Technology)


Risk Management Framework(RMF)
 .Prepare: Activities that are necessary to manage security and privacy risks before a breach occurs

 .Categorize: Create tasks to how to deal with it.

 .Select: Gather documentation about the controls that protect an organization, such as a playbook

 .Implement: Implement security and privacy plans.

 .Assess: Determine if established controls are implemented correctly

 .Authorize: Be accountable for the risks that may exist in the organization

 .Monitor: Be aware of how systems are operating and if they are achieving the goals of the org.
